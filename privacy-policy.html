Privacy Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2025 · Last Updated: April 2, 2025

Overview

Qibla Compass ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application and any associated services.

🔒 Summary: Qibla Compass does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no data collection.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Location history or GPS coordinates
  • Crash logs or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or other device data

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's GPS location. This is the only permission the App requires.

Your location data is used exclusively and solely to:

  • Calculate the Qibla direction (bearing toward the Kaaba in Makkah) from your current position
  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your geographic location
📍 Your GPS coordinates are processed in real time, entirely on your device. They are never transmitted to any server, stored in any database, or shared with any third party — including us. Once you close the App, no location information is retained anywhere.

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The App will continue to function in a limited capacity without location access.

Device Sensors (Compass)

The App optionally accesses your device's compass (magnetometer) and accelerometer to provide a real-time rotating Qibla needle. On iOS, this requires explicit user permission.

Compass and motion sensor data is used only to rotate the on-screen needle in real time. This data is processed locally on your device and is never stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

Qibla Compass does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs.

There are no:

  • Advertisements or ad networks
  • Analytics SDKs (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Crash reporting tools (Sentry, Crashlytics, etc.)
  • Social login or third-party authentication
  • In-app purchases or payment processors

Children's Privacy

Qibla Compass does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). The App does not collect any personal data from any user, regardless of age, making it safe for use by people of all ages.

Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no personal data to secure on our end. All computations — including Qibla direction and prayer time calculations — are performed using open mathematical algorithms running entirely within the App on your device.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, export, or delete. You have full control over your device permissions at all times through your device's operating system settings.

To manage the App's permissions on your device:

  • iPhone (iOS): Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services
  • Android: Settings → Apps → Qibla Compass → Permissions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
